HayMaker Stem Expo

  • Home
  • ILITE Events
  • HayMaker Stem Expo
Come join us on Saturday, February 6th, 2016 to play, build, make, and explore in the 2nd annual HayMaker Science, Technology, Engineering and Math (STEM) Expo.

The expo will run from 10AM to 5PM with movies Big Hero 6 and October Sky playing at 11AM and 3PM respectively. Students and enthusiasts of all ages and skills (elementary, middle, high school and even our “Golden Years” students) are invited to share our vision and to revel in a gathering of technology enthusiasts, crafters, tinkerers, hobbyists, engineering, science clubs, sustainability concepts, students and local exhibitors. Those interested in learning of our full offerings for “STEM’ers” can visit here for a list of activity based exhibits and entertainment list and here for school sponsored exhibits (including colleges).

This is an extraordinary vision that encompasses:

  • Student presentations, and demonstrations and a moment to show off their hard work
  • Makers and Shakers that are bringing back the concept that people can ‘make’
  • Sustainable concepts that allow people to do for themselves: gardening, composting, off grid, electric cars, renewable energy, conservation, recycle
  • Leaders in the STEM industry: from small business to the large

The expo will boast a wide variety of family-friendly STEM activities, concessions and informational booths for ILITE summer camps and Battlefield High School’s IT program as well as countless local businesses and organizations. If you are interested in participating as a maker or exhibitionist, you can let us know more about you and your proposed project by applying here. If you are an interested in volunteering for the expo, you can sign up here. Admission has been waived and is free of charge. To learn more, visit the expo’s website.

We would love it if you could come share this amazing experience with us!
