FTC COMPETITION

  • Home
  • ILITE Events
  • Regional FTC Competition

ILITE Robotics is hosting a Regional FTC (FIRST Technical Challenge) Robotics Qualifier as a way of promoting the amazing experience that we have had with FIRST to our community on December 12th, 2015. We will be playing this years game, FIRST RES-Qsm. Children of all ages will be able to come together and enjoy the new, exciting event. From all over our community students will gather together, and have the chance to test the robots and the programs they had put together.

In order for success in such a venture, it is imperative to have a great volunteer staff. Thank you to all for your continued support of our next generation. A sincere “Thank you” from all of us at ILITE.
